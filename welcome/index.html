Welcome aboard

This is your spot to park little pages and share them with people.

How it works

Every page you publish lives in its own folder in the repo. The homepage automatically finds every folder and lists it — newest first — so you never have to update an index by hand. Make a thing, push it, and it shows up.

Adding a new page

You don't do this part by hand. Just tell Claude:

push this to randomphonebuilds

Claude turns whatever you're working on into a clean page in this same style, drops it in a new folder, and pushes it. About a minute later it's live and on the homepage.

A word on the password

The homepage asks for a password (letmesee), but treat this whole site as public — anyone with a direct link to a page can open it, and the files are readable on GitHub. It's fine for notes, guides, and builds. Keep contracts, invoices, and anyone's private info off of it.
